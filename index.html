hi, my name is Kristof.

you may risk reading my shorter thoughts.

if they survive long enough.

you could also try to laugh at some of my comic strips.

it happens from time to time, but don't try to hard.

or marvel at my sketch and doodle stuff.

your reasons to marvel are yours, and yours alone.

your eyeballs might enjoy some of the pictures i take.

no guarantees you won't go blind though.
